Support

We're here to help with gameplay issues, billing questions, and privacy requests.

Contact us

For all support inquiries, email us at:

info@andalusgames.com

We aim to respond within 2–5 business days. Please include your device model, OS version, game title, and a description of the issue.

Common topics

  • Gameplay bugs — describe what happened and steps to reproduce.
  • Ads not loading — check your internet connection and ad tracking settings on your device.
  • In-app purchases — refunds are handled by Apple App Store or Google Play per their policies.
  • Account / progress loss — most games store progress locally on your device unless cloud save is enabled.

Privacy & data requests

To request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data, email info@andalusgames.com with the subject line "Privacy Request". See our Privacy Policy for details on your rights.
