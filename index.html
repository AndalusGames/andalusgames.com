Who we are

Independent studio.
Mobile-first mindset.

AndalusGames is an independent mobile game studio focused on arcade-style experiences — quick to pick up, hard to put down. Every title is built in Unity and tuned for touch controls, short sessions, and high replay value.

From desert dagger challenges to cop chase drift racing, our games blend accessible mechanics with polished mobile monetization and platform-ready builds for Android and iOS.
