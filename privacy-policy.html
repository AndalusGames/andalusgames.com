Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026 · Effective for all AndalusGames mobile applications

AndalusGames ("we", "us", or "our") operates mobile games published under the AndalusGames brand. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you use our games on Android, iOS, or other platforms, and when you visit andalusgames.com.

1. Information we collect

Depending on the game and features you use, we and our third-party partners may collect:

  • Device information — device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, language, and country.
  • Advertising identifiers — such as Google Advertising ID (GAID) or Apple Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA), where permitted by your device settings.
  • Gameplay data — scores, progress, session length, in-app events, and crash reports to improve stability.
  • Technical logs — IP address, app version, and diagnostic data collected automatically for analytics and fraud prevention.
  • Support communications — information you send when contacting us at info@andalusgames.com.

We do not knowingly collect personal information that directly identifies a child under 13. Our games are not directed at children under 13.

2. How we use information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve our games and services.
  • Display advertisements and measure ad performance.
  • Analyze usage trends and fix bugs or crashes.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.
  • Respond to support requests and user inquiries.

3. Third-party services

Our games may integrate third-party SDKs that collect data under their own privacy policies, including but not limited to:

  • Google AdMob — Google Privacy Policy
  • AppLovin MAX — AppLovin Privacy Policy
  • Unity Analytics / Ads — Unity Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — platform services governed by their respective policies.

We encourage you to review the privacy settings on your device, including options to limit ad tracking and reset advertising identifiers.

4. Legal basis (EEA / UK users)

Where applicable, we process personal data based on: (a) your consent (e.g. personalized ads where required); (b) legitimate interests in operating and improving our games; and (c) compliance with legal obligations.

5. Data retention

We retain information only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law. Analytics and advertising partners may retain data according to their own schedules.

6. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is consent-based.
  • Opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information (where applicable under CCPA/CPRA).
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@andalusgames.com. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe as required by applicable law.

7. International transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. We take appropriate safeguards where required by applicable data protection laws.

8. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect changes. Continued use of our games after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
